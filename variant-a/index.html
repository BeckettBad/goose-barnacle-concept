The Family

Family run, community inspired.

Step through the shop's antique wooden doors and you will find husband and wife team David Alperin and Michelle Lorenzutti welcoming you in with a smile.

David opened the store back in 2010, continuing a family legacy of running businesses on Atlantic Avenue.

Brooklyn Roots · La Coruña

A hundred years on Atlantic Avenue.

David's grandmother, Emma Sullivan, immigrated to Brooklyn from La Coruña, Spain in the late 1920's and the family has lived in the Cobble Hill-Brooklyn Heights area ever since.

In 1951, Sullivan opened The Long Island Bar, a diner on Atlantic Avenue.
